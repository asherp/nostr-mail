# German BIP39 Wordlist

## Provenance

This German wordlist (`german.txt`) is sourced from the [dys2p/wordlists-de](https://github.com/dys2p/wordlists-de) repository, specifically the `de-2048-v1.txt` file.

The wordlist contains 2048 German words and follows BIP39-compatible formatting (one word per line, lowercase).

**Source URL:** https://raw.githubusercontent.com/dys2p/wordlists-de/refs/heads/main/de-2048-v1.txt

## Official BIP39 Standard

**Important Note:** The official BIP39 standard (as defined in [BIP-0039](https://github.com/bitcoin/bips/blob/master/bip-0039.mediawiki)) does **not** include a German wordlist among its officially supported languages.

The official BIP39 wordlists currently include:
- English
- Japanese
- Korean
- Spanish
- Chinese (Simplified)
- Chinese (Traditional)
- French
- Italian
- Czech
- Portuguese

This German wordlist is a community-maintained alternative that follows BIP39 principles but is not part of the official Bitcoin BIPs specification. Use at your own discretion and ensure compatibility with your specific use case.
